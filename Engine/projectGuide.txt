----PLAY SCENE----

--AUDIO--
Pressing "1", "2", and "3" keys will play an audio file.


--PHYSICS--
LEFT SHIFT + P will toggle step mode. In step mode, physics will update on user input only
0 Key: Update physics if step mode is toggled.
I Key: Apply upwards force on first rigid body.
K Key: Apply downwards force on first rigid body.
J Key: Apply force to the left on first rigid body.
L Key: Apply force to the left on first rigid body.
O Key: Resets velocity and forces acting on the rigid body.
